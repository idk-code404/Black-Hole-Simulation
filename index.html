Black Hole Physics

⚫ Singularity

The central point of a black hole where matter is compressed to infinite density and zero volume. All known physical laws break down at this point.

🔴 Event Horizon

The boundary around a black hole beyond which nothing can escape, not even light. This is the "point of no return" where escape velocity exceeds light speed.

🟡 Accretion Disk

A disk of superheated gas and dust spiraling into a black hole. Friction heats the material to millions of degrees, emitting intense radiation.

🌀 Photon Sphere

A region where gravity is so strong that light photons travel in orbits around the black hole. These orbits are unstable - small perturbations cause photons to either escape or fall in.

🌌 Gravitational Lensing

The bending of light around massive objects due to spacetime curvature. This effect creates multiple images, arcs, or rings of background objects.

🍝 Spaghettification

The vertical stretching and horizontal compression of objects into long, thin shapes due to extreme tidal forces near a black hole.
